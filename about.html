About Artify

A classy Bootstrap mini‑gallery created for coursework. Uses public‑domain images via Wikimedia Commons.

Features

  • 10 pages: Home, Art Display, Login, Sign Up, About, 5 individual art pages.
  • Dark, elegant theme with gold accents and high‑quality images.
  • Carousel hero, hover animations, fade‑in on scroll, search filter.

All artwork images are from Wikimedia Commons (Public Domain) using Special:FilePath links.
